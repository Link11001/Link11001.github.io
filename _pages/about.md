- **Marine Time Series Forecasting**

## 🚀 About Me

I am committed to combining **deep learning** and **remote sensing techniques** to explore problems related to **multi-source remote sensing data understanding**, **marine environment perception**, and **time series modeling**.

In my research, I am particularly interested in **hyperspectral remote sensing**, **SAR / LiDAR data fusion**, **intelligent ocean information processing**, and **model design and optimization for real-world applications**.
